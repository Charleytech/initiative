Our Story

A journey of compassion and commitment.

From a Simple Idea to a Movement

The Gela Helping Hand Initiative started in 2018 with a small group of friends in Lagos, Nigeria, who were moved by the daily struggles of families in their local community. They began by pooling their own resources to provide meals and school supplies for children who couldn't afford them.

Word of their good deeds spread, and soon, more volunteers and small donations came in. We realized the need was far greater than we could have imagined. This humble beginning blossomed into a registered foundation with a clear mission: to provide sustainable support and create lasting change.

Expanding Our Reach

Having made a significant impact across various states in Nigeria, we are now driven by a powerful vision to extend our helping hands to other African nations. We believe that poverty knows no borders, and neither should compassion. Our goal is to partner with local communities across the continent to empower the poor and build a brighter future for all.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable individuals and communities, particularly women and children, through targeted grants, education, and healthcare support, fostering self-reliance and sustainable development across Africa.

Our Vision

To create a continent where every person has the opportunity to live a life of dignity, free from poverty, and is empowered to reach their full potential.
